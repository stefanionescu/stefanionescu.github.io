About Me

Hi, I'm Stefan! Currently I'm building two mobile games called Tiles and BackPack. I've been in the tech space (with a focus on crypto) since late 2017 when I started falling in love with Ethereum. In 2020, I cofounded and led the development of RAI which is a crypto stable asset managed by a PID controller. At its peak, the RAI protocol had more than $390M in total value locked. I was also one of the main contributors to Astroport which used to be one of the largest decentralized exchanges in the world (average daily volume of $300M, total value locked peaked at $1.7 billion).

Prior to RAI and Astroport, I used to dive into Ethereum scaling solutions and build infrastructure that connects traditional finance to DeFi. In another life, I was a mobile app developer.
